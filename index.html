Research

US Counterterrorism Spending

In this research project, we investigated the factors that motivate the US counterterrorism budget. Using historical budget data, we demonstrate how this spending has drastically increased following the attacks on 9/11 and has remained high even as the number of attacks and casualties from terrorist attacks has declined in both the United States and by Foreign Terrorist Organizations abroad.
